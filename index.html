How to view a source page

Term: Under the Sea

  1. Click on the link of a website you want to visit
  2. For mac, right click on the webpage
  3. Click on the tab that says "View Source Page
  4. You will then see the raw html page that shows the structure of the website
  5. You can also repeat step 3 and instead click inspect
  6. Hover the mouse over different elements of the website
  7. You will see a side by side representation of the website and html
